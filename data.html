Data documentation archive

The following datasets and data repositories have been used by Near Space Interface in some of its projects. Below you will find the name of the dataset, repository or sensor used for its creation, the entity in charge of creating and managing the datasets, the period of time in which the data is available, a short description, the format in which they can be downloaded and the links to download the document with the necessary information for downloading, organizing, cleaning, previewing and visualizing the datasets, as well as links to additional documentation.

Most of the data used by NSI is Open Data, that is, data that can be freely used, re-used and redistributed by anyone*. However, it can also be open-access data, which still requires registration and complex protocols for its use**.

* Open Data Handbook - What is Open Data?
